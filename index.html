Privacy & Data Safety

Your privacy is our priority. WorthNot is designed to work entirely on your device without compromising your personal data.

Local Notification Processing

When a bank or UPI notification arrives, we only extract the amount and merchant. This data is processed locally and never uploaded to any server.

Zero Data Collection

We do not collect or store your phone number, name, bank account details, or transaction history on any external database.

Transparency

The Notification Access permission is used solely to provide instant reflection alerts. You can revoke this permission at any time from your system settings.

Developer Commitment

WorthNot is committed to the Google Play Developer Policy. We only request permissions necessary for the app's core functionality: helping you track and reflect on your digital spending.

Data Safety Declaration

  • No data shared with third parties.
  • No data collected in the cloud.
  • Encrypted local storage for your goals.
